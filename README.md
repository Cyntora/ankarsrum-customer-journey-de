# Ankarsrum, Customer Journey, Germany (Draft)

Single-market deep dive of the customer journey on Ankarsrum's German market, delivered by Cyntora. Germany is positioned as a mature market alongside Sweden.

**Market:** Germany (`ankarsrum.de`)
**Period:** 15 May 2025 to 14 May 2026
**Version:** 1.0 (DE draft, fourth market after Sweden, USA and UK; built on the v1.1 pattern)
**Delivered:** May 2026

## Scope

This deck is a data-driven foundation for the German market. It describes how Germany behaves in the channels and analytics that are measurable today (GA4, GSC, Meta Ads, Google Ads, Shopify). It is intended as an input to the strategy agency's continued customer journey, persona and positioning work, not as a replacement for that work.

## Notes on the data

- Germany is a separate domain (`ankarsrum.de`) and reports in EUR, unlike the `.com/<market>/` subfolders used by the other markets.
- GA4 records 1,599 transactions for the .de domain over the period. The dedicated German Shopify store is smaller on its own; the domain also routes some checkout through a shared European store. GA4 is the on-domain figure used throughout the deck.
- The German Meta account is billed in SEK as part of central billing, so Meta revenue is shown in SEK while site revenue is in EUR. ROAS and purchase counts are currency-neutral.
